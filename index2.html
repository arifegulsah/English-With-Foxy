Who We Are?

As the English with Foxy team, our mission is to provide free world-class English education to all Turkish children around the world. The reason for being a non-profit organization is that we support Turkish children everywhere to get a free English education, and we aim to make them represent Turkey worldwide. Our team, consisting of volunteer university students who study in different branches and have advanced English language skills, aims to give lessons that will provide children with the highest level of efficiency. The lessons are planned to develop grammar, "reading", "writing", "listening", and "speaking" skills of children.
